Enjoying Time with Family


In my father's spare time, he enjoys the time he gets to spend with his family. Whether it is spending time around the home, working outside, or going on vacations, Mr. Andrew prefers to do things with his family over anyone else. Of the people in his world, his family is the most important to him.

Zoos and Aquariums


The places that my father enjoys the most are museums, zoos, and aquariums. Joey Andrew has a thirst for knowledge, and loves to learn of history and of God's creatures around him. Of all the places he has been, he remembers the ones he visisted with his family the most.

God's Animals


On our travels, the Andrew family has seen a great many different animals in different zoos and aquariums across the country. Of these, my father has enjoyed poison dart frogs, butterflies of all varieties, and the ruby throated hummingbird the most. He enjoys the bright colors of the frogs, the beauty of the butterfly, and the magestic flight patterns that Hummingbirds can take. As a hobby at home, Mr. Andrew has several hummingbird feeders that he enjoys watching during down time.
